Brother printer series

Which Brother printer series do you have?

⚡

Brother HL series

Monochrome laser printers for home and office use. HL models are simple, fast, and economical for text-heavy printing. Common issues include offline errors after deep sleep mode activates and toner cartridge recognition errors with third-party toner.

🖨️

Brother MFC series

Multi-function center printers combining print, scan, copy, and fax in inkjet and laser configurations. MFC models are the most widely used Brother printers in home offices. Common issues include scanner driver failures on Mac and wireless dropout when the printer enters energy saving mode.

📋

Brother DCP series

Digital copier and printer models without fax, available in inkjet and laser. DCP printers offer print, scan, and copy in a compact form factor. Common issues include ADF paper feed errors and offline status after Wi-Fi password changes.

🏢

Brother WorkSmart series

Business laser printers with advanced network features including gigabit Ethernet, high monthly duty cycles, and multi-user support. Common issues include IP address conflicts on large networks and driver compatibility after enterprise Windows updates.

🎨

Brother InkBenefit Plus

Ink tank inkjet printers for low cost per page, positioned similarly to Epson EcoTank. InkBenefit Plus models use bottled ink refills. Common issues include printhead clogging after inactivity and ink tank overflow during refilling.

🔖

Brother P-touch Label Printers

Dedicated label printers using thermal printing or laminated tape. P-touch models connect via USB, Bluetooth, or Wi-Fi. Common issues include driver installation on Windows 11 and compatibility with Brother P-touch Editor software after OS updates.

Common Brother issues

Step-by-step fixes for the most reported Brother printer problems.

1

Brother printer offline — deep sleep fix

Brother printers enter deep sleep after a configurable period of inactivity. In deep sleep, the printer disconnects from the network and appears offline to connected devices. To fix the immediate issue: press any button on the printer to wake it, then try printing again. To prevent recurrence: access the printer menu, go to General Setup, Ecology or Sleep Mode, and increase the sleep timer or disable deep sleep entirely. On MFC models, this setting is under All Settings, General Setup, Ecology, Sleep Mode.

2

Brother wireless setup

On Brother laser and inkjet printers with a display panel, go to Settings, Network, WLAN, Setup Wizard. Select your Wi-Fi network and enter the password. Alternatively, use WPS: on the printer, go to Settings, Network, WLAN, WPS with PIN Code or WPS button method. Press WPS on your router within 2 minutes. The Brother iPrint and Scan app also provides guided wireless setup from iOS or Android with automatic network detection.

3

Brother drum replacement

Brother laser printers use a separate drum unit and toner cartridge. The drum unit (not the toner cartridge) needs replacement approximately every 12,000 to 30,000 pages depending on the model. When the drum warning appears, the drum is nearing the end of its life but can often continue printing for several weeks. To replace: open the front cover, remove the toner and drum assembly, press the green tab to separate the toner from the drum, install the new drum, and reinsert the toner cartridge. After replacement, reset the drum counter from the printer menu.

4

Brother scanner not working on Mac

Brother scanners require the ICA scanner driver for macOS, in addition to the printer driver. Download both the printer driver and the ICA driver from support.brother.com. After installing both, go to System Settings, Printers and Scanners. The scanner should appear as a separate device or be accessible through the printer properties. If the scanner still does not respond, check that macOS Privacy settings grant scanning permissions to the app you are using.

Brother error messages

Common Brother error messages and how to resolve them.

Replace Toner or Toner Low

Brother toner cartridges show a low warning with several hundred pages remaining. You can continue printing by holding the Go button for 10 seconds when the Toner Low message appears, which extends use until the cartridge is truly empty. When Replace Toner appears, the cartridge is fully depleted and must be replaced.

Use high-yield TN cartridges rather than standard yield for better cost per page on high-volume printers.

Unable to Print or Init Unable

This error occurs when the drum or toner cartridge is not installed correctly or the front cover is not fully closed. Open the front cover, remove and reinsert the toner and drum assembly, and close the cover firmly until it clicks. Check that the blue locking lever on the drum is in the locked position.

If the error continues after reinstalling, the drum unit may be damaged or at end of life.

No Device Found (Mac)

When adding a Brother printer on Mac and it shows No Device Found, the printer driver may not be installed or the printer is on a different network. Confirm the printer is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as the Mac. Reinstall the full driver package from support.brother.com. In the printer add dialog, try adding by IP address if automatic discovery does not find it.

Brother offers a Network Connection Repair Tool for Mac that automatically diagnoses and fixes most connection issues.

Paper Jam error with no paper

Open all access panels including the back cover and duplex tray if installed. Check for any small paper fragments lodged in the paper path or around the drum area. Use a flashlight to inspect carefully. Remove all fragments before closing panels. Run a test print after clearing to confirm the jam is fully resolved.

Recurring paper jams often indicate worn paper feed rollers. Clean rollers with a lint-free cloth or request roller replacement.
