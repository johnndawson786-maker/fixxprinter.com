Epson Printer Troubleshooting by Series — Epson Printer Setup Help

🛢️

Epson EcoTank

The EcoTank series uses large refillable ink tanks instead of cartridges. EcoTank models have very low per-page costs and are ideal for frequent home or office printing. Common issues include clogged print heads after long periods without printing and air bubbles in the ink system after refilling.

🏢

Epson WorkForce

Business-focused inkjet AIO printers with auto duplex, ADF, and network printing. WorkForce models are commonly used in small offices. Common issues include offline status after router changes and scanner driver failures after macOS updates.

🏠

Epson Expression

Home-focused inkjet printers for photo and document printing. Expression models include photo-quality models with additional ink colors. Common issues include ink cartridge errors after using third-party cartridges and Wi-Fi setup problems on networks with hidden SSIDs.

📸

Epson SureColor

Professional photo and wide-format printers for photographers and design professionals. SureColor models use multiple ink channels for precise color reproduction. Common issues include ink head cleaning cycles that consume significant ink and media feed calibration for specialty papers.

📄

Epson EcoTank Pro

Extended EcoTank models with business features including auto duplex, ADF, Ethernet connectivity, and high-yield tank capacity. Common issues are the same as standard EcoTank models plus network configuration for large office environments.

🖥️

Epson ET and XP series

The ET series covers current EcoTank models and the XP series covers mid-range home inkjets. Both are among the most commonly owned Epson printers. Common issues include Wi-Fi band compatibility (2.4 GHz only) and driver download confusion between similar model numbers.

Epson Printer Offline & Error Codes — Epson Printer Troubleshooting Reference

Error code 0x97

A hardware error typically related to the main board or print head. Turn off the printer, unplug it for 5 minutes, hold down the paper feed and power buttons while plugging back in, then release after 3 seconds. If the error persists, it may require a hardware service.

This error is most common on older WorkForce models and may indicate internal component failure.

Ink pad is at end of service life

Epson printers collect waste ink in a pad inside the printer. When the pad is full, the printer stops printing and displays this message. The pad must be replaced or the counter reset using Epson Adjustment Program software. This is normal maintenance on printers used for several years.

The reset procedure varies by model. Contact us with your Epson model number for the correct procedure.

Paper jam error with no paper

If the printer shows a paper jam but no paper is visible, a small fragment may be caught in the roller mechanism. Power off the printer, open all access panels, and inspect carefully using a flashlight. Gently remove any paper fragments without tearing them further inside the mechanism.

After clearing, run the printer self-test to confirm the rollers are clear before sending a print job.

Printer not recognized on Mac after update

macOS updates occasionally remove printer driver compatibility. Go to System Settings, Printers and Scanners. Remove the Epson printer. Check Software Update for a new Epson driver, or download directly from epson.com. Re-add the printer after the driver installs.

Epson also releases macOS compatibility updates through its own website, sometimes before Apple distributes them.
